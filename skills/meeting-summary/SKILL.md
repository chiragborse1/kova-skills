# Meeting Summary

When given a meeting transcript or notes, produce a structured summary with exactly three sections:
1. **Decisions Made** — what was agreed upon
2. **Action Items** — who needs to do what by when  
3. **Key Discussion Points** — main topics covered

Keep each section to bullet points. Be concise.
